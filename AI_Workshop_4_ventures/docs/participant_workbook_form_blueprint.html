Participant Workbook Form Blueprint

Concrete Google Forms blueprint for the participant-owned workbook workflow.

Form structure overview

SectionPurposeQuestion types
1. Start and identityLet the learner start from a personalized entry point and understand the privacy model.Short answer, multiple choice, consent note
2. Readiness and comfortCapture device/account readiness and comfort/support signals.Multiple choice, checkboxes, short answer
3. Choose your activityPick category and level with low-choice framing.Multiple choice with branching
4. Guided prompt workCapture starter prompt, improved prompt, and what changed.Paragraph, short answer
5. Output reviewCapture expectation versus result and the remaining gap.Multiple choice, paragraph
6. Adaptive follow-upRoute to Guided, Build, Remix, or Stretch follow-up prompts.Section branching plus paragraph answers
7. Reflection and takeawaysCapture what worked, what surprised the learner, and next steps.Paragraph, checkboxes
8. CompletionProvide a closing note and optional summary-email choice if enabled.Multiple choice, email field if used

Recommended question wording

  • What name, initials, or private label should we use for your workbook?
  • What topic do you want to work on tonight?
  • Which path would help you most right now: Guided, Build, Remix, or Stretch?
  • Which activity do you want to start with?
  • What weak starter prompt did you begin from?
  • What changed in your improved prompt?
  • Did the result meet what you hoped for: yes mostly, partly, or not yet?
  • What gap remained?
  • What would you still verify?
  • What will you try next?
  • If you finished early, what extension did you try?

Branching design

Decision pointBranching rule
Guided selectedSend to narrower sentence-frame prompts and one small follow-up step.
Build selectedSend to slightly more open prompt revision tasks.
Remix selectedSend to audience/tone or format adaptation follow-ups.
Stretch selectedSend to comparison, extension, or reflection-plus-remix tasks.
Result = Not yetShow an extra coaching question before moving on.
Need help flaggedMark the response for leader follow-up in Sheets.

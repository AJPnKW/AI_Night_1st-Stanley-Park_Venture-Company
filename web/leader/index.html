Leader overview for session support and safeguarding awareness.

This page is intentionally low-profile. It exists for leaders who already have the link and need concise oversight notes.

Important: this is an independent volunteer-created support page, not an official unit or Scouts Canada operational system.

Leader role

  • Maintain calm structure and visible support.
  • Help youth re-enter the task if they lose track.
  • Escalate safety, wellbeing, or tech concerns early.

Session oversight cues

  • Check who needs clarification, not just who raises a hand.
  • Watch for overload, social pressure, or frustration spikes.
  • Normalize breaks and quieter participation.
  • Redirect unsafe data-sharing immediately.

Simple logistics

  • Confirm devices, chargers, connectivity, and seating.
  • Keep a clear start-middle-end verbal structure.
  • Use direct language about what happens next.
  • Keep public-facing messaging aligned with the independent-site disclaimer.

# Content Strategy

Content is written to be:

- youth-appropriate without sounding childish
- parent-readable without becoming corporate
- specific and directional
- low-jargon and low-ambiguity

Content tiers:

- Participant content explains the event flow and next actions.
- Parent content explains purpose, tools, consent, privacy, and confidence signals.
- Shared content handles glossary, examples, and FAQ.
- Leader and facilitator content stay concise and low-profile.

No lorem ipsum, filler copy, or “24th” references are retained in final delivery.
